Wanted Poster Assignment

What is it?

For this assignment, the class had to divide into groups of 4 or 5 people and had to create an identification page for the class in a whacky theme. Group 4 decided to make Wanted Posters. To go about this, we had to take headshots of everyone in the class and then turn them into Wanted Posters with our names and student numbers using software such as Photoshop.
